# Region Evolution Paths - Complete Design

This document defines the 7 major regions and their evolution paths, including enemy rosters, bosses, loot profiles, and environmental themes.

---

## 1. Northern Realm

**Base Theme:** Nordic/Viking-inspired region. Starting tier always 1 when chosen as origin.

### Path 1: Age of Raiders (Expansion & Conquest)
- **Tier 1:** Small bandit encampments, wolf packs, fishing villages, scattered settlements
- **Tier 2:** Organized raider clans, naval expansion, merchant caravans, active trade routes
- **Tier 3:** Regional settlements with political power, warbands with leadership hierarchy
- **Tier 4:** Emergent empire with military infrastructure, fortified cities, standing armies
- **Tier 5:** Raider civilization—trade dominance, naval supremacy, cultural expansion

**Enemies by tier:**
- T1: Bandit, Wolf, Ice Goblin, Hunter
- T2: Veteran Bandit, Raider, Ice Shaman, Frost Wolf
- T3: Huskarl, Berserker, Ice Troll, Valkyrie
- T4: Frost Giant, Ancient Jarl, Runic Bear, Ice Dragon
- T5: Fenrir, Jörmungandr (sea serpent), Valhalla Champion, Ethereal Raider

**Bosses:** Warlord Jarl (T2), Frost King (T3), Ragnar the Eternal (T4), Jörmungandr Avatar (T5)

**Loot:** Viking weapons (axes, swords), fur armor, stolen treasures, trade goods, merchant records

**Visual Identity:** Wooden settlements, longships, torches, warm interiors contrasting cold exterior

**Factions:** Raider Clans, Merchant Guilds, Northmen Settlements

---

### Path 2: Eternal Winter (Environmental Collapse)
- **Tier 1:** Unseasonable frost, scattered ice creatures emerging
- **Tier 2:** Permanent snow blankets the land, migration patterns disrupted, ancient frozen structures
- **Tier 3:** Temperature drops further; pre-historical creatures thaw from glaciers
- **Tier 4:** Entire biome frozen; temporal distortions manifest
- **Tier 5:** Mythological winter awakens—ice gods walk the earth

**Enemies by tier:**
- T1: Frost Sprite, Ice Stalker, Permafrost Golem, Chilled Shade
- T2: Blizzard Wraith, Glacial Beast, Primordial Mammoth, Frozen Specter
- T3: Ancient Frost Dragon, Primordial Giant, Eternal Snowborn, Icebound Titan
- T4: Cryogenic Leviathan, Frost God Avatar, Temporal Wraith, Endless Blizzard (summonable)
- T5: Skadi (goddess), Eternal Winter Incarnate, Primordial Ice, Chronos of the North

**Bosses:** Cryomancer (T2), Primordial Frost Dragon (T3), Winter God Emissary (T4), Skadi Herself (T5)

**Loot:** Cryogenic weapons (ice-imbued), winter furs, ancient frozen artifacts, temporal shards, mythic relics

**Visual Identity:** Glaciers, aurora borealis, frozen ruins, snowstorms, aurora-lit dungeons, time-distorted architecture

**Factions:** Shamans of Winter, Survivors, Temporal Keepers (new faction)

---

### Path 3: Ragnarök (Mythological Awakening)
- **Tier 1:** Mysterious omens, runestones glow, prophets emerge
- **Tier 2:** Giants awaken, prophecies begin to manifest
- **Tier 3:** Bifrost bridge solidifies, celestial events occur
- **Tier 4:** All nine realms become accessible; beings from each emerge
- **Tier 5:** The World Tree grows; cosmic war erupts

**Enemies by tier:**
- T1: Runestone Guardian, Prophecy Manifestation, Omen Crow, Mythic Awakening
- T2: Frost Giant, Fire Giant, Storm Giant (variants), God's Herald
- T3: Demi-gods, Celestial Warriors, Bifrost Walkers, Valkyrie Squads
- T4: Full Gods (Odin variant, Thor variant), Cosmic Entities, World Tree Sentinels
- T5: Surtr (fire giant lord), Jörmungandr (world serpent), Fenrir (giant wolf)

**Bosses:** Harbinger of Ragnarök (T2), Surtr's Emissary (T3), Odin's Chosen (T4), Fenrir or Surtr (T5)

**Loot:** Legendary mythic weapons (Gungnir variants), divine armor, runes of power, fate threads

**Visual Identity:** Ancient Norse temples rebuilt, celestial rifts, divine light, mystical runes glowing everywhere, interconnected realm gateways

**Factions:** Prophets of Fate, Norse Gods (new NPC faction), Survivors seeking redemption

## Summary & Usage

Each region has 4 unique paths, creating 28 distinct evolution scenarios. The paths are balanced to have:
- **Early-tier identity** (T1-2): unique but accessible
- **Mid-tier escalation** (T3): signature bosses and themes emerge
- **Late-tier culmination** (T4-5): path flavor reaches apotheosis

Paths are designed to:
1. Be mechanically distinct (different enemy archetypes, loot, encounters)
2. Tell coherent stories (progression feels narratively driven)
3. Interact with each other (e.g., Undead spreading from one region to adjacent)
4. Reward replay (different path = different content and strategy)

For the simulator, use these as seed data to test tier spreading, path assignment probabilities, and novelty metrics.
